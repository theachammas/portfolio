DATA PORTRAIT

A couple of years ago, when I got extremely ill, the doctors asked me to keep a diary tracking down my hunger and thirst cues to find out exactly what was wrong with me and save my life. And it actually did work. So I decided to work with this idea as it was the most personal one and track my cues throughout the whole week. Each time I got hungry or thirsty, I wrote it down in my notes (with the time of the cue), and wrote the level of it on a scale of 1 to 4. I then calculated the average levels daily, and worked on my sketch accordingly.
